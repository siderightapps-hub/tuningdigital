Response Times

  • Editorial & partnerships — usually within 2 business days.
  • Privacy & data requests — within 30 days, as required under GDPR.
  • Security reports — acknowledged within 5 business days.

What We Don't Do

  • Paid placement in rankings — vendors cannot purchase better positions in our comparisons or best-of lists.
  • Unbranded review trades — we won't write a positive review in exchange for a backlink, free product, or any other benefit.
  • Bulk PR pitches — generic press releases not tied to a specific tool we cover are unlikely to get a reply.
