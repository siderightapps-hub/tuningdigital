## Phase 3: Backlink Acquisition (Ongoing)

### Tier 1: Easy Wins (Do First)

**Free tool directories** — Submit the SaaS Cost Calculator to:
- Product Hunt (https://producthunt.com) — schedule a launch
- There's An AI For That (https://theresanaiforthat.com)
- Futurepedia (https://futurepedia.io)
- AI Tools Directory (https://aitoolsdirectory.com)
- Toolify (https://toolify.ai)
- Priceful (https://priceful.app)
- SaaSHub (https://saashub.com)

**Expected:** 5–20 DA 30–60 backlinks from directory submissions alone.

**Startup / bootstrapper communities:**
- IndieHackers — share articles in the "Growth" section
- Hacker News — submit well-researched tool comparisons (Show HN)
- Reddit — r/SaaS, r/productivity, r/artificial, r/ChatGPT (be genuinely helpful, not spammy)

### Tier 2: Outreach (Month 2–3)

**Journalist / blogger outreach:**
1. Find listicle posts ("best AI tools 2025") ranking on page 1–2
2. Email authors noting you have a more updated/detailed comparison
3. Offer a quote, unique data, or a custom calculator they can embed

**HARO / Connectively:**
- Sign up at https://connectively.us
- Reply to queries about AI tools, SaaS, productivity, startup software
- Consistent HARO responses → 1–3 DR 50+ links/month

**Podcast guesting:**
- Pitch yourself to podcasts in: SaaS, productivity, founders, AI
- Talk about AI tool selection, stack audits, SaaS spend
- Podcast episodes → backlink in show notes

### Tier 3: Strategic (Month 3–6)

**Original research / data:**
- Survey 200+ knowledge workers on their SaaS stack (use Typeform free tier)
- Publish "State of AI Productivity Tools Survey 2025"
- Pitch findings to TechCrunch, VentureBeat, The Information
- Expected: 5–20 high-DA links from news coverage

**Tool vendor programmes:**
- Many SaaS companies have affiliate + partner programmes that include a listing in their "Built with X" or "Integrations" pages
- Reach out to Notion, Zapier, HubSpot about co-marketing
- These links are DR 70–90 and come with referral traffic

**Guest posting targets (DA 50+):**
- SaaStr.com (SaaS focused)
- TechCrunch.com (pitch as contributor)
- HackerNoon.com (free self-publish, indexed)
- Dev.to (developer productivity angle)
- Medium.com/Towards AI publication
- AppSumo blog

## Domain Valuation Drivers (for resale)

To maximise resale value of tuningdigital.com:
1. **Age + backlinks** — every month of age with incoming links increases value
2. **Monthly organic traffic** — aim for 10k+ sessions/month before listing
3. **Revenue documentation** — AdSense + affiliate revenue must be proven with 6–12 months of screenshots
4. **Email list** — a newsletter list of 1,000+ is a significant multiplier
5. **Content volume** — 50+ indexed articles signals active site
6. **Clean technical SEO** — no Google penalties, fast loading, mobile-friendly

**Valuation benchmark:** Sites in this niche typically sell for 30–40× monthly net profit on Flippa/Empire Flippers. A site earning £500/month could sell for £15,000–£20,000.
