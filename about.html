About Us

We Find the Signal.
You Skip the Noise.

Tuning Digital is an independent publication covering AI and SaaS productivity tools. We test software hands-on, publish honest reviews, and help teams make smarter buying decisions.

🔬

Hands-On Testing

Every review is based on real usage — not vendor demos or press releases.

⚖️

No Pay-to-Rank

Rankings are based entirely on merit. Vendors cannot buy better placement.

🔄

Weekly Updates

The AI tool landscape moves fast. We update reviews when products change significantly.

What We Cover

We focus on the productivity and automation layer of the modern software stack — the tools that knowledge workers, founders, developers, and small teams use every day to get more done:

  • AI writing and research tools — Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, Jasper
  • Project management and productivity — Notion, Linear, Asana, ClickUp
  • No-code automation — Zapier, Make, n8n
  • Design tools — Figma, Framer, Canva
  • Analytics and SEO — GA4, Ahrefs, SEMrush, Plausible
  • CRM and sales — HubSpot, Attio, Pipedrive

How We Make Money

Tuning Digital is supported by three revenue streams, all disclosed transparently:

  • Affiliate commissions — When you click a link and purchase a tool, we may earn a small commission. This never influences our rankings or editorial opinions.
  • Display advertising — We run Google AdSense ads on some pages. Ads are clearly labelled and we have no control over which ads are shown.
  • Sponsored content — Occasionally we publish clearly-labelled sponsored posts in partnership with vendors. These are always marked "Sponsored" and do not influence our independent reviews.

We believe honest disclosure is the foundation of trust. If a link earns us a commission, we say so. If content is sponsored, we label it clearly.

Our Review Methodology

Each tool review follows a consistent framework:

  1. We sign up for the tool using a paid plan (or the highest available free tier)
  2. We use it for a minimum of 7–30 days on real tasks
  3. We run standardised benchmark tasks to enable fair cross-tool comparison
  4. We document pricing, limitations, and edge cases from the current plan page — not archived data
  5. We update reviews when pricing, features, or performance changes materially
