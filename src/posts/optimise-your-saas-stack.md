# How to Optimise Your SaaS Stack

Most teams pay for more tools than they need—and use only a fraction of the features.

## Step 1: Inventory your tools

- List every subscription and its monthly cost
- Note the **primary job** each tool does
- Identify overlapping features

## Step 2: Tune before you switch

- Explore advanced settings and automation
- Consolidate workflows into fewer tools
- Remove unused seats and add-ons

## Step 3: Review quarterly

A quick quarterly review keeps your stack lean, fast, and aligned with what you actually use.
