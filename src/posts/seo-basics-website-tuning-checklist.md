# SEO Basics: A Website Tuning Checklist

Search optimisation doesn't have to be complicated. Start with the fundamentals and you’ll already be ahead of most sites.

## Technical foundations

- Make sure your site loads over **HTTPS**
- Use a **fast, mobile-friendly** layout
- Check that important pages return **status 200**, not 404 or 302

## On-page essentials

- One clear **H1** per page
- Descriptive **title tags** and **meta descriptions**
- Internal links to your most important pages

## Content tuning

- Focus each page on a **single main topic**
- Use headings and bullet points for readability
- Answer the questions your audience actually searches for
