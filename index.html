How a tool earns its dial

    We pay full price
    Every subscription is bought at retail on a normal plan. No press seats, no comped enterprise tiers.

    Same criteria, every time
    Speed, reliability, pricing fairness, support, security, and a handful more — weighted identically across categories so a 4.5 means the same thing in AI as it does in SEO.

    Real work, not vendor demos
    We ship actual client work with each tool for weeks before writing a single line of the review. Demos lie; production rarely does.
  • 04
    One calibrated number
    The criteria collapse into a single score out of five — and we re-test when products ship major changes. The score isn't a snapshot, it's a commitment.
